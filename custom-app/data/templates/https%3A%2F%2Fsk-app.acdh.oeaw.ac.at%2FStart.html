The SemanticKraus Project

The Austrian satirist Karl Kraus shaped Viennese culture from the Fin de Siècle to the end of the First Republic and has had a lasting impact ever since; research on him – ‘traditional’ as well as digital – is rich and growing. In the course of the CLARIAH-AT funded research project SemanticKraus (October 2022 to December 2023), research data gained from different edition projects on Karl Kraus was accumulated and transformed to RDF. This platform allows the exploration of this data. You may start your exploration by chosing one of our selected samples, or you can just use the search bar below. This platform also provides a SPARQL interface (see the SPARQL page). The SemanticKraus project invites other digital research and/or edition projects to contribute to a Linked Data Cloud centered around Karl Kraus, his work and his era (see Contact Page).
